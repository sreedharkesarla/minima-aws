Warranty Information

Standard Warranty:
All products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

What's Covered:
- Hardware malfunctions
- Software defects
- Battery issues (first year only)

What's NOT Covered:
- Physical damage from drops or accidents
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs
- Normal wear and tear

Extended Warranty Options:
- 3-Year Extended: $49.99
- 5-Year Extended: $89.99  
- Includes accidental damage protection

Filing a Claim:
1. Contact customer service within 30 days of issue
2. Provide proof of purchase
3. Describe the problem in detail
4. Ship product using prepaid label

Processing Time:
- Standard repair: 5-7 business days
- Replacement: 3-5 business days
- Refund: 10-14 business days

For questions, contact warranty@example.com
